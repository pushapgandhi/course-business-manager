# Learning Material Quality Checklist

## Purpose

Use this checklist before approving student-facing learning materials, worksheets, quizzes, assignments, rubrics, or certificate criteria.

## Source Workbook Checks

- The Course Content Planning Workbook exists.
- The requested row, lesson, module, or batch is marked Approved.
- The handoff ID is present.
- The lesson or module objective is clear.
- Key concepts are listed.
- The practice activity or assignment is defined.
- The student output is defined.
- Success criteria are present or marked as a curriculum gap.
- Any curriculum change requests are sent back to Curriculum before production.

## Student Material Checks

- The material matches the approved objective.
- Instructions are student-facing and easy to follow.
- Key concepts match the workbook.
- The activity or worksheet produces the approved student output.
- The material does not introduce new modules, lessons, or outcomes.

## Quiz Checks

- Questions test the approved objective and key concepts.
- Questions are clear and answerable from the lesson material.
- The answer key is complete.
- Explanations help students understand the correct answer.
- Passing criteria are defined or marked for approval.

## Assignment And Rubric Checks

- Assignment instructions match the approved workbook item.
- Deliverables are specific.
- Success criteria are observable.
- Rubric criteria align with the approved student output.
- Performance levels are clear enough for consistent review.

## Certificate Criteria Checks

- Completion rules match approved course outcomes and assessment style.
- Required evidence is observable.
- Passing thresholds are clear or marked for approval.
- Certificate wording does not overstate what the course proves.

## Approval Summary Format

```text
Learning material review:
- Workbook source approved: Yes/No
- Student material ready: Yes/No
- Quiz/answer key ready: Yes/No
- Assignment/rubric ready: Yes/No
- Certificate criteria ready: Yes/No
- Alignment issues:
- Curriculum revision needed: Yes/No
- Recommended next action:
```
