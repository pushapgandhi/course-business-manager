# Teaching Notes Template

## Purpose

Use this template to create recording support notes for one approved lesson.

Teaching notes help the instructor record the video smoothly. They should emphasize pacing, examples, mistakes to avoid, and delivery reminders instead of duplicating the full video script.

## Instructor Reminders

- Keep the lesson tied to the workbook objective.
- Define terms before using them in examples.
- Pause after the practice prompt so students know what to do.
- Avoid adding new curriculum topics during recording.
- Mention the student output before the recap.

## Teaching Notes Quality Check

- Do the notes match the PowerPoint slide titles?
- Do the notes support the video script without replacing it?
- Are pacing and emphasis clear?
- Are common mistakes and clarifications included?
- Is the instructor warned not to introduce unapproved curriculum changes?
