# Lesson Production Checklist

## Purpose

Use this checklist before approving lesson content assets for recording.

## Source Workbook Checks

- The Course Content Planning Workbook exists.
- The requested row, lesson, module, or batch is marked Approved.
- The handoff ID is present.
- The lesson objective is clear.
- Key concepts are listed.
- The example or demonstration is defined.
- The practice activity is defined.
- The student output is defined.
- Any curriculum change requests are sent back to Curriculum before production.

## Presentation Checks

- The PowerPoint presentation follows the approved lesson objective.
- Slide titles match the video script.
- Each slide has a clear purpose.
- On-screen text is short enough for video.
- Visual directions are clear for screenshots, diagrams, examples, or demos.
- The practice slide tells students exactly what to do.

## Video Script Checks

- The script is slide-by-slide.
- Each slide has on-screen text, voiceover, and visual direction.
- The voiceover is natural and beginner-friendly.
- The example matches the workbook.
- The recap reinforces the lesson objective.
- The next step matches the workbook assignment or checkpoint.

## Teaching Notes Checks

- Teaching notes match the presentation and video script.
- Notes include pacing, emphasis, and common mistakes.
- Notes support recording instead of duplicating the full script.
- Notes do not add new modules, lessons, or outcomes.

## Approval Summary Format

```text
Lesson production review:
- Workbook source approved: Yes/No
- PowerPoint ready: Yes/No
- Video script ready: Yes/No
- Teaching notes ready: Yes/No
- Alignment issues:
- Curriculum revision needed: Yes/No
- Recommended next action:
```
