The script should tell the instructor exactly what to say while recording the course video.

## Script Quality Check

- Does the script stay aligned to the approved workbook row?
- Does the voiceover sound natural when read aloud?
- Does each slide have visible content, narration, and visual direction?
- Does the practice activity create the approved student output?
- Does the recap connect to the next lesson or assignment?
