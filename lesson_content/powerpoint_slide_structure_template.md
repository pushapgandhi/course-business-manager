# PowerPoint Slide Structure Template

## Purpose

Use this template to create a PowerPoint `.pptx` presentation for recording one approved course lesson.

The presentation must match the approved Course Content Planning Workbook row and the matching video script.

## Recommended File Name

```text
[course-name]-[handoff-id]-[lesson-title]-presentation.pptx
```

## Slide Structure

| Slide | Slide Purpose | Required Content |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Title and hook | Lesson title, hook, and quick reason this lesson matters |
| 2 | Lesson objective | What students will understand or be able to do |
| 3 | Concept overview | Main concept in simple language |
| 4 | Step-by-step explanation | Process, framework, or decision path |
| 5 | Example or demonstration | Workbook-approved example, case, screen, or scenario |
| 6 | Practice activity | Student task, prompt, or short exercise |
| 7 | Common mistakes | Likely mistakes and what to do instead |
| 8 | Recap | Key takeaways |
| 9 | Homework or next step | Assignment, checkpoint, or next lesson bridge |

## Slide Content Rules

- Use short on-screen text that supports the spoken explanation.
- Keep one main idea per slide.
- Prefer bullets, tables, screenshots, simple diagrams, or examples over dense paragraphs.
- Match slide titles exactly with the video script sections.
- Include visual direction when the final presentation still needs screenshots, diagrams, or examples.

## Slide Draft Format

```text
Slide number:
Slide title:
On-screen text:
Visual direction:
Speaker cue:
Workbook source:
```

## Quality Check

- Does every slide support the approved lesson objective?
- Does the example match the workbook?
- Does the practice activity produce the approved student output?
- Is each slide recordable without extra planning?
- Do slide titles match the video script and teaching notes?
