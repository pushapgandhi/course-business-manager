# Communication Message Templates

These templates are starting points. Customize every message with the user's context before approval.

## Cold Outreach Email

Subject options:

- Quick question about [goal/problem]
- Idea for [recipient/company/audience]
- Helping [audience] with [outcome]

Draft:

```text
Hi [Name],

I noticed [specific context or reason for reaching out].

I help [audience] achieve [outcome] through [course/program/service]. If improving [pain point] is relevant right now, I can share a simple next step or resource.

Would it be useful if I sent over [resource/idea/details]?

Best,
[Sender]
```

## Lead Follow-Up Email

```text
Hi [Name],

Following up on [conversation/context].

Based on what you shared about [goal or pain point], the next useful step would be [recommended action].

Would you like me to [CTA]?

Best,
[Sender]
```

## WhatsApp Follow-Up

```text
Hi [Name], quick follow-up on [context].

The next step is [CTA or reminder]. Let me know if you want me to send the details here.
```

## LinkedIn DM

```text
Hi [Name], thanks for connecting.

I work on [course/business topic] for [audience]. I noticed [specific context], and thought [resource/idea/offer] may be useful.

Open to me sending a short note with details?
```

## Student Welcome Message

```text
Hi [Name],

Welcome to [Course/Program].

Your main goal for this course is [student outcome]. The first step is [first action], and the important date to remember is [date/time].

If you have questions before starting, reply here and I will help.

Best,
[Sender]
```

## Class Reminder

```text
Hi [Name],

Reminder: [Class/Session name] is scheduled for [date/time] at [location/link].

Please bring or complete [prework/materials], if applicable.

See you there,
[Sender]
```

## Payment Or Deadline Reminder

```text
Hi [Name],

This is a quick reminder that [payment/deadline/action] is due by [date/time].

You can complete it here: [link]

Let me know if you have any questions.

Best,
[Sender]
```

## Testimonial Request

```text
Hi [Name],

I am glad you completed [course/program/milestone].

Would you be open to sharing a short testimonial about your experience? A few lines on what changed for you, what you learned, and who you would recommend this to would be very helpful.

If easier, you can reply using this format:

- Before the course, I was struggling with:
- The most useful part was:
- After the course, I can now:

Thank you,
[Sender]
```

## Meeting Confirmation Message

```text
Hi [Name],

Confirming our meeting for [date/time] about [topic].

Best,

```

```text

Best,
```
