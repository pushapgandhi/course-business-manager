## Minimal Intake Questions

If details are missing, ask only what is needed:

1. What calendar action should I take: schedule, reschedule, cancel, update, remind, or prepare?
2. Who should attend?
3. What date/time or time range should I use?
4. What timezone should I use?
5. How long should the meeting be?
