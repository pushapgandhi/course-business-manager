# Community Engagement Templates

Use these templates for student community posts, anonymized answers, weekly prompts, accountability, and wins.

## Weekly Progress Prompt

```text
Weekly check-in:

What did you complete this week?
What are you stuck on?
What is your next small action?

Post your answer in the comments. If you are stuck, share the exact step and I will help you choose the next move.
```

## Anonymized Student Question Post

```text
A student asked a useful question this week:

"[Anonymized question]"

Short answer:
[Clear answer]

Try this:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

If you are stuck on the same thing, reply with your version and I will help you improve it.
```

## Win Sharing Prompt

```text
Share one win from this week.

It can be small:
- finished a lesson
- submitted an assignment
- clarified your niche
- booked a call
- improved your draft

Post the win and the step that helped you get there.
```

## Accountability Prompt

```text
Accountability thread:

Pick one task you will complete before [day/date].

Use this format:
"By [date], I will complete [specific task]."

Keep it small enough to finish.
```

## Lesson Discussion Prompt

```text
Discussion for [module/lesson]:

What was the most useful idea from this lesson?
Where could you apply it in your own course or business?
What part still feels unclear?
```

## Community Rules Draft

```text
Community guidelines:

1. Ask specific questions so others can help.
2. Share drafts and progress before asking for broad feedback.
3. Give practical, respectful feedback.
4. Do not share another student's private work outside the group.
5. Keep promotion, spam, and unrelated links out of the community.
6. If something is urgent or private, use the support form instead of posting publicly.
```
