# Student Support Reply Templates

These templates are starting points. Customize every reply with the student's actual question, course context, and next action.

## Course Question Reply

```text
Hi [Student Name],

Thanks for the question. You are asking about [topic/module], and the main thing to focus on is [core idea].

Here is the simplest next step:

1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

Try this and send me [specific output or answer]. I will help you adjust it from there.

Best,
[Sender]
```

## Stuck Or Confused Student Reply

```text
Hi [Student Name],

It makes sense that this part feels unclear. [Briefly name why this topic is tricky].

Do not try to solve everything at once. For now, complete this one step:

[Specific exercise, question, or action]

Once you have that, reply with [specific thing to send back]. I will help you tighten the next part.
```

## Assignment Feedback Reply

```text
Hi [Student Name],

I reviewed your work on [assignment/task].

What is working:
- [Positive observation]

What to improve:
- [Specific improvement]

Next action:
[Clear revision step]

After you update it, send the revised version and I will check whether it is ready for the next lesson.
```

## Motivation Or Inactivity Reply

```text
Hi [Student Name],

I noticed you may be stuck around [module/lesson/task].

You do not need to catch up on everything today. The best next step is:

[Small action that can be completed quickly]

Reply once it is done, or tell me what is blocking you. I will help you choose the next step.
```

## Complaint Or Frustration Reply

```text
Hi [Student Name],

Thanks for telling me directly. I understand that [summarize the issue without arguing].

To help properly, I need to separate this into:

1. What happened: [known facts]
2. What you expected: [student expectation]
3. What we can do next: [next action or escalation]

I will review this and come back with a clear next step. If there is a policy or access issue involved, I will escalate it before giving a final answer.
```

## Technical Or Access Issue Reply

```text
Hi [Student Name],

Thanks for flagging this. To troubleshoot the access issue, please send:

1. The link or page where the issue happens
2. A screenshot of the error
3. The device/browser you are using

In the meantime, try [simple safe troubleshooting step, if known].

Once I have those details, I can help identify the next step.
```
