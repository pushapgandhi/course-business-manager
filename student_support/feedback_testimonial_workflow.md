# Feedback And Testimonial Workflow

## Purpose

Use this workflow to collect student feedback, improve the course, and request testimonials or case studies from students who report useful results.

## Feedback Collection Workflow

1. Choose the feedback moment: after a lesson, module, live session, milestone, or course completion.
2. Ask focused questions instead of broad satisfaction questions.
3. Separate course-improvement feedback from public testimonial requests.
4. Summarize recurring feedback into course-improvement notes.
5. Track whether the issue affects content, delivery, support, community, sales expectations, or operations.

## Feedback Form Questions

```text
Name:
Course/cohort:
Module or lesson:
What was most useful?
What was confusing or missing?
What did you complete or improve because of this lesson?
What should be added, removed, or explained better?
How confident do you feel about applying this? Low / Medium / High
May we follow up about your result or feedback? Yes / No
```

## Testimonial Candidate Criteria

A student is a testimonial candidate when at least one is true:

- completed the course or major milestone
- achieved a clear result
- gave positive feedback with specific details
- shared a before/after change
- improved a project, skill, confidence, or business outcome
- volunteered appreciation or public praise

## Testimonial Request Template

```text
Hi [Student Name],

I am glad to hear that [specific result or milestone].

Would you be open to sharing a short testimonial about your experience with [course/program]? It would help future students understand what the course is like and what kind of progress is possible.

If helpful, you can reply using this format:

- Before the course, I was struggling with:
- The most useful part was:
- After the course, I can now:
- I would recommend this to:

Only share what you are comfortable making public. I will show you the final version before using it.

Thank you,
[Sender]
```
