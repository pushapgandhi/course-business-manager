## Safeguards

- Do not promise employment, income, grades, promotions, or guaranteed business results.
- Do not use testimonials unless the user provides them.
- Do not create fake urgency.
- Do not mention refund or guarantee terms until approved.
- Keep answers direct and beginner-friendly.
