## Publishing Checklist

- Final offer approved
- Final price approved
- CTA destination approved
- Refund policy approved
- Guarantee approved or intentionally omitted
- Testimonials are real and user-provided
- No unsupported outcome, income, employment, or revenue claims
- Payment link approved before use
- Page URL approved before sharing
