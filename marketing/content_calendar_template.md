## Status Rules

- Idea: topic is captured but not drafted.
- Draft: copy exists but has not been reviewed.
- Review: ready for user review.
- Approved: user approved the content.
- Designed: visual asset is complete.
- Scheduled: post is scheduled in Meta Business Suite or another scheduler.
- Posted: post is live.
- Repurpose: post should be reused in another format or platform.
