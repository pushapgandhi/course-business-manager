# Repurposing Workflow

## Purpose

Use this workflow to turn one approved source asset into many platform-specific marketing assets without rewriting from scratch.

## Source Asset Types

Good source assets include:

- approved lesson script
- course module summary
- student workbook section
- worksheet or activity
- student question
- approved testimonial or case study
- webinar outline
- lead magnet
- newsletter draft
- blog post
- recorded video transcript

## Workflow

1. Identify the source asset and approval status.
2. Extract the main idea, audience pain point, practical insight, and CTA.
3. Choose the primary Instagram format.
4. Create the Instagram content packet.
5. Create Canva-ready visual directions.
6. Create secondary repurposed assets for LinkedIn, YouTube, newsletter, blog/SEO, or lead magnet use.
7. Run the quality checklist.
8. Prepare scheduler-ready fields.
9. Track performance after posting and mark strong posts for repurposing.

## Repurposing Map

| Source Asset | Instagram Carousel | Instagram Reel | Stories | LinkedIn | YouTube | Newsletter | Blog/SEO | Lead Magnet |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lesson script | Key steps or framework | 30-60 sec teaching clip | Quiz or question sequence | Authority post | Short or full video idea | Teaching section | How-to outline | Checklist |
| Student question | Mistakes and answer | Quick answer reel | Poll + answer | Advice post | FAQ video | Reader Q&A | FAQ article | Mini guide |
| Workbook activity | Step-by-step carousel | Demo reel | Completion prompt | Practical how-to | Tutorial | Practice prompt | Tutorial outline | Worksheet |
| Testimonial | Proof post | Story/reel only if media exists | Social proof story | Case-study angle | Student story topic | Success note | Case study outline | Proof section |
| Offer details | Objection carousel | Offer explainer | Waitlist prompt | Launch post | Webinar topic | Promo email | Sales article | Opt-in CTA |

## One Asset To One Week Example

| Day | Asset |
|---|---|
| Monday | Carousel teaching the main framework |
| Tuesday | Story poll about the audience problem |
| Wednesday | Reel explaining one mistake |
| Thursday | Single post with a strong belief or myth |
| Friday | Lead magnet or waitlist CTA |

## Quality Rules

- Change the format for each platform; do not paste the same copy everywhere.
- Keep Instagram content visual and concise.
- Keep LinkedIn content more authority and story driven.
- Keep newsletters more personal and useful.
- Keep blog/SEO outlines searchable and structured.
- Use testimonials only when the user provides exact testimonial content.
- Route direct messages, outreach, and email sending to the Communication Agent.
