| Type | Best For | Example |
|---|---|---|

| Build task | Producing a reusable asset | Draft a lesson outline or workflow checklist |
| Review task | Improving quality through critique | Audit your module map for missing outcomes |

## Capstone Design Rules

- The capstone should prove the main course outcome.
- The capstone should combine multiple course skills, not test a single isolated lesson.
- The required deliverable should be clear enough that students know when they are done.
- The success criteria should be practical and observable.
- The capstone should fit the course level and time available.

## Review Format

```text
Assignment/capstone review:
- Does it connect to a learning outcome?
- Is the deliverable clear?
- Is the required effort realistic?
- Are success criteria observable?
- Does the task prepare the student for the next course or business use case?
```
